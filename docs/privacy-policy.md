# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

## What we collect
- Account profile data.
- Usage, feedback, and quality signals.
- Conversation content needed to operate the service.
- Billing, subscription, and abuse-prevention metadata.

## What we do not do
- We do not train foundation models automatically on raw user data.
- We do not expose secrets in dashboards or logs.

## User controls
- Export your data.
- Delete your account.
- Disable conversation improvement.
- Clear stored memory.

## Retention
- Conversations, logs, analytics, feedback, and deleted-account data are retained only as long as needed for service, safety, and compliance.
